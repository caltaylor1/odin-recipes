Spicy chicken burrito

Spicy chicken burritos are incredibly tasty and also very easy to make!
You have the choice of eating it hot or cold which is great if you're fancing something nice the next day for your work dinner.

Please see notes section at the bottom of the page prior to starting!

Ingredients

  • Deboned roast chicken - 1kg (see note at bottom)
  • Tomatoes - 2x large (skin removed and roughly chopped)
  • Diced onion x1
  • Minced garlic x4 tsp
  • 3-4 red chillies (finely sliced)
  • 2x Bay leaves
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • Salt
  • 2x green chillies (chopped)
  • 250ml sour cream
  • 6x 10inch tortillas
  • 250g grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil to medium temperature in a skillet.
  2. Add the onions and fry until translucent.
  3. Add bay leaves, garlic and red chillies, fry for 1 minute.
  4. Add the tomatoes, sugar, pepper and salt.
  5. Fry for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and take out bay leaves.
  7. Place the soured cream and green chillies in a blender, proceed until smooth.
  8. Warm the tortillas in the oven.
  9. Shred the chicken

Putting it together

  1. Divide the shredded chicken onto the tortillas
  2. Spoon the sauteed tomato and chilli sauce over
  3. Sprinkle with cheese and top with soured cream
  4. Wrap, serve and devour!

Notes

If you were making the tortillas to eat hot roast the chicken according to the package instructions prior to starting the beginning setps.
* If serving cold burritos allow sauce to cool before covering.
This recipe serves 6 portions.
